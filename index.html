About me

Hey, I'm so glad you're here! My name is Yaru Luo, but you can call me Ru (she/her). I am currently pursuing a PhD in experimental optical physics at CU Boulder in the Scott Diddams Lab. I graduated with two BAs in English & Physics at Pomona College and honors in my theses. I grew up in NYC and am now based in Colorado. I like working with children, snowboarding, contributing to teams, rock-climbing, and listening to rock or indie music when I code. I conduct research in laser frequency combs, with a focus in extreme precision radial velocity calibration instrumentation. I am also deeply passionate about the language of protest and radicalism, social justice, and equity in STEM. I'm a Yankees fan and love to read the works of Toni Morrison and contemporary autofiction authors. It's nice to meet you!
